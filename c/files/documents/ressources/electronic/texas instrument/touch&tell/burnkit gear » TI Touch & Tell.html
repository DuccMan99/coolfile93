Phoneme loop lock and loop timbre controls:
This freezes the sound currently being produced by
the speech synth creating a very short loop.
Depending on what sound yourâe creating, you
might get anything from an abrasive crashing to a delicate theremin type sound. The timbre pot fine
tunes the loop points of your phoneme which can change your tone from smooth to aggressive. Connect pin 1 of CD2802NL to the center post of an audio tapered 10K pot. Connect one end post of the pot to a SPST switch, putting 7.4K worth of resistors between them. Connect pin 13 to the other leg of your SPST.

I also used 2 trimmer pots to fine tune the pitch extremes with some fresh batteries in it. Theyâre connected by their center legs to the main tuning pots outer posts.
