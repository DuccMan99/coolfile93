SKM Version 2: Assembly Notes

Important Information

This kit contains some ESD-sensitive parts. Please take reasonable static-control precautions when assembling the kit.

PCB Layout

The location of each part is clearly marked on the SKM circuit board.

Parts List

All components are mounted on the side of the board with the white legend. Components can be mounted in any order, but it is easier to mount the smallest components first.

Part Image* Notes
C1-C2None.
C3 This part is not required.
D1-D9Dark band on diode must match the white band on the circuit board legend.
IC1Mount socket on the circuit board first, then insert IC into socket. Notched end must match notch in the circuit board legend.
IC2Mount socket on the circuit board first, then insert IC into socket. End marked with dot must match notch in the circuit board legend.
R1220Ω resistor, marked with the following color bands: red, red, brown, gold.
R210kΩ resistor, marked with the following color bands: brown, black, orange, gold.
SW1With the circuit board oriented as in the photo above, switch 1 should be on the left.
*Appearance of parts may vary.
