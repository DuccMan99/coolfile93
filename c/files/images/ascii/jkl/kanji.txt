Hiragana-----A phonetic spelling of words (ka ki ku ke ko....)
Katakana-----Like hiragana but for nonjapanese words(KA KI KU KE KO...)
Kanji--------Derived from Chinese conceptual writing,Mainly
symbols              stand for words,or ideas
