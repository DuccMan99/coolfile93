From: David Palmer



Here's the 1973 GT Beetle that I owned for 4 years.
The GT was a limited edition VW launched to try to boost falling sales.
It was a 1300 body shell with flat windsheild and [ shaped bumpers
(fenders). The gearbox (transmission) was the 1300 close ratio
manual box bolted to a 1584cc flat four air-cooled engine.
Other GT extras were the half padded dashboard and the special
wheels which I think were also on the 1974 Jeans Beetle which
featured the blue demin interior.
