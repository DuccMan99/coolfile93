This was an experiment if you will. It was a compilation ascii-art
project. One person did a portion of the picture and set the theme and
then passed it on to the next person. When all 5 people did their part
of the scene, then they all took turns in diddling each others work.

Here is the latest in the Nursary Rhyme Series. The series is a compilation
project. I gather a few artists and we do different parts of a rhyme and
combine our individual efforts into one pic. The artists used in of "Bah,
Bah, Black Sheep" were JRO, Krogg, jgs, mga, & dwb.

All the rhymes in the series so far are displayed below.
